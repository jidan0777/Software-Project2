About US

Jidan.com, one of Bangladeshi's best known brands and the world's largest matrimonial service was founded with a simple objective - to help people find happiness. The company pioneered online matrimonials in 1996 and continues to lead the exciting matrimony category after more than a decade. By redefining the way Bangladeshi brides and grooms meet for marriage, Jidan.com has created a world-renowned service that has touched over 35 million people.
